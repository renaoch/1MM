Our Story

BORN IN
GUWAHATI

From a small stall idea to Assam's most talked-about momo destination. This is the story of 1MM.

01
The Obsession

It started with a simple obsession — why couldn't momos be as exciting as any other street food? We spent months perfecting ratios, testing sauces, and folding hundreds of dumplings until we found it: the perfect momo.

02
The Secret Sauce

Our 14-ingredient sauce recipe is our most guarded secret. Each ingredient plays a role — heat, depth, brightness, umami. Together they create something that can't be replicated. Our regulars can recognise the taste blindfolded.

03
Veg Always

We made a deliberate choice: 100% vegetarian, always. This lets us focus on perfecting vegetable-based fillings instead of being mediocre at many things. Our veg momos beat most non-veg competitors — that's our quiet pride.

04
The Community

1MM isn't just a restaurant. It's become a gathering spot for Guwahati's students, foodies, and families. With 960+ followers and 222+ reviews, every plate we serve is a new friendship made over food.

How We Got Here
2019
The Beginning

1MM opens its doors at Kumarpara, KRC Road, Guwahati. Word spreads fast — there's something genuinely different happening here.

2020
The Billionaire Menu

We launched the Billionaire Menu with the Freyo Tower — 8 unique momos in one iconic serving. It sold out every weekend.

2022
Pizza Momo Goes Viral

Our flame-gun Pizza Momo became a sensation on Instagram. Hundreds of reels, thousands of views. The momo world would never be the same.

2024
4.5 Stars & Growing

With 222+ Google reviews and a loyal community, 1MM stands as one of the best-rated momo joints in Guwahati. We're just getting started.
